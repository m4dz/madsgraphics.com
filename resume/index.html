Book

I wouldn't be MAD if I wasn't making another cool project (damned! where does he find all of this time?), So I'm currently writing a book called CSS Collaboratives : Méthode d'organisation durable, performante et responsive, with the support of the delicious ladies and awesome guys of Wagon 42. It tries to give a solution to the huge and unmaintainable large stylesheets, especially when you work on a large codebase with multiple developers.

It presents a method that I developed, called STICSS, that is just an organisational method to write and collaborate around your CSS.

I hope the book can be edited in english sooner after the french version :).
